Moana

"Moana" is a 2016 animated musical film produced by Walt Disney Animation Studios. The film is set in ancient Polynesia and follows the journey of a young girl named Moana Waialiki, who sets sail on a daring mission to save her people.

Story: Moana, the daughter of the chief of the island of Motunui, is chosen by the ocean to restore the heart of Te Fiti, a goddess who created all life in the islands. The heart was stolen by the demigod Maui, causing darkness to spread across the ocean.
